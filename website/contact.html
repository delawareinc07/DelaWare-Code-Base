FAQ

Common Questions

How quickly can you start?

Typically within 1–2 weeks of a signed agreement. For urgent projects, we can often start sooner.

Do you work with early-stage startups?

Yes. We work with companies at every stage, from pre-revenue to Series C. We adjust our approach to what makes sense for your stage.

What industries do you specialize in?

We've worked across 45+ industries. Our methodology transfers well, and we bring relevant case studies to every engagement regardless of vertical.

What does a typical engagement look like?

Most projects start with a discovery phase (1–2 weeks), move into an execution phase (4–12 weeks depending on scope), then transition to a support or retainer arrangement if needed.
